Official Privacy Policy

ResuMate

Application Package: com.resumate.app | Last Updated: July 3, 2026

1. Privacy Commitment & Offline-First Principles

Welcome to ResuMate ("we," "our," or "the Application"). We are deeply committed to protecting your personal privacy and maintaining complete data security. ResuMate is designed as an offline-first application—your personal resume content, employment history, contact information, and documents remain stored locally on your device unless explicitly exported or synchronized by you.

Key Privacy Promise: ResuMate does not sell, rent, monetize, or track your personal resume data or contact details to third-party data brokers or advertisers.

2. Information Stored & Handled

ResuMate handles information strictly to enable resume creation, formatting, and AI analysis:

  • Local Resume Data: Full Name, Email, Phone Number, Work Experience, Education, Skills, and Custom Sections entered by you are saved in isolated device local storage (SharedPreferences).
  • Optional Authentication Data: If you choose to sign in via Google Sign-In / Firebase Authentication, we store your basic profile identity (display name, email address, profile picture URL) strictly to manage your account session.
  • AI Request Data: Raw resume text or prompt queries sent to the Google Gemini API for resume extraction or ATS scoring are processed ephemerally by Google's API to generate structured suggestions.
  • API Keys: User-provided Google Gemini API keys are saved locally on your device and are never transmitted to any external servers other than official Google AI endpoints.

3. How Your Information Is Used

The information handled within ResuMate is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Generating and rendering 8 ATS-optimized PDF and Microsoft Word (.docx) resume files.
  • Providing intelligent AI resume parsing, ATS scoring, and bullet-point suggestions.
  • Creating encrypted offline backup files (.resumate) for 1-tap data restoration.
  • Maintaining authenticated user sessions via Firebase Auth when enabled.

4. Third-Party Services & Integrations

ResuMate integrates with trusted Google developer services to power specific features:

  • Google Play Services: Core application execution, APK delivery, and updates.
  • Firebase Authentication: Secure user sign-in and session management (Firebase Privacy Policy).
  • Google Gemini API: Generative AI resume extraction and analysis (Google Privacy Policy).

We do not integrate with third-party tracking SDKs, ad networks, or invasive analytics tools.

5. Data Control & Deletion Rights

You have full ownership and control over your data at all times:

  • In-App Deletion: You can edit or delete individual resumes at any time directly within the ResuMate dashboard.
  • Complete Wipe: Clearing app data or uninstalling ResuMate from your Android device immediately and permanently deletes all stored resume data and local backups.
  • Account Sign-Out: You can sign out of Google / Firebase authentication anytime via the account menu.

6. Security Measures

ResuMate implements industry-standard security practices to safeguard your data. All network communications with Google APIs use TLS/HTTPS encryption. Backup files utilize schema-signature validation to prevent file tampering or corruption.

7. Children's Privacy

ResuMate is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us for immediate removal.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy periodically. Any updates will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" revision date.
